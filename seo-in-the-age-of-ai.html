Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)

GEO is the practice of optimizing content to directly influence and appear within AI-generated search results, like Google's AI Overviews. The goal is to be the source material the AI uses to construct its answers.

AI Optimization (AIO)

AIO is the broadest strategy, encompassing all efforts to make content discoverable and influential within any AI conversation. This includes not just search engines, but also dedicated chatbots like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Claude.

Key Tenets of AIO

  • Prompt Readability: Structuring content so LLMs can easily parse and summarize it.
  • Establishing Trust: Building strong E-E-A-T signals so your site is chosen as a reliable source.
  • Anticipating Intent: Creating content that answers the complex, multi-turn questions common in conversational AI.

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)

AEO is a specialized discipline focused on securing "Position Zero" placements. This means providing concise, authoritative answers that appear in Featured Snippets, People Also Ask boxes, and voice search results.

AEO Strategies

  • Be Concise: Provide direct answers in 40-60 words, perfect for snippets.
  • Use Structure: Employ lists, tables, and Q&A formats.
  • Leverage Schema: Use structured data (FAQPage, HowTo) to give search engines explicit clues.
  • Target Questions: Focus on the long-tail, conversational questions real users are asking.

The Modern SEO Playbook

Theory is important, but execution is key. This playbook provides actionable strategies for winning in the AI era. Click on each section to expand and reveal the best practices for content creation, technical optimization, and establishing authority.

  • Adopt Modular Content: Build content in "Lego blocks" that can stand alone as answers or combine into a comprehensive guide.
  • Format for Answerability: Use question-based headers (H2s, H3s), bullet points, numbered lists, and TL;DR summaries.
  • Prioritize Intent Over Keywords: Focus on comprehensively answering the user's underlying question, not just stuffing keywords.
  • Use Hybrid Content: Combine text with high-quality, unique images, infographics, and videos to provide richer context for both users and AI.
  • Ensure Crawlability & Indexing: If Google can't find your content, it can't be used in an AI Overview. A clean `robots.txt` and XML sitemap are vital.
  • Prioritize Site Speed: Fast-loading pages provide a better user experience, a key signal for all ranking systems.
  • Implement Structured Data: Use Schema markup (FAQPage, Article, etc.) to explicitly tell AI what your content is about, reducing ambiguity.
  • Maintain Strong Site Architecture: Logical internal linking helps AI understand the relationships between content on your site and distributes authority.

E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) is the ultimate currency for AI. AI models are designed to find and cite the most reliable sources.

  • Show, Don't Just Tell: Demonstrate first-hand experience with real-world examples, case studies, and original data.
  • Cite Reputable Sources: Link out to authoritative studies, government reports, and academic papers to back up claims.
  • Establish Author Hubs: Create detailed author bios showcasing credentials and expertise.
  • Build Your Brand Entity: Earn mentions from other trusted sources to build a strong brand footprint that AI can recognize.
